# QuestCode JS Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-05

QuestCode JS ("we", "our", "us") provides a JavaScript learning mobile app and supporting backend services.
This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how users can control it.

## Data We Collect

- Account data: email address, username, hashed password.
- Learning progress: path selection, lesson progress, XP, streak, exercise history.
- Competitive data: duel rating, duel session outcomes, leaderboard placement.
- Technical/session data: auth tokens issued to signed-in users.

## Data We Do Not Intentionally Collect

- Government-issued identifiers.
- Payment card data.
- Precise geolocation.
- Contact lists, photos, microphone, or camera media (unless explicitly added in a future release and disclosed here).

## How We Use Data

- To authenticate users and secure accounts.
- To provide learning progression, onboarding, streaks, and personalized path state.
- To power realtime duels and ranking systems.
- To operate, debug, and improve reliability and security.

## Legal Basis and Consent

- We process account and progress data to provide the core app service.
- If optional analytics, marketing, or tracking are added in the future, we will request explicit opt-in consent before collection.

## Data Sharing

- We do not sell personal data.
- We only share data with infrastructure/service providers required to run QuestCode JS (for example, hosting and database providers), subject to contractual confidentiality and security requirements.

## Data Retention

- Account and progress data are retained while an account is active.
- Users may request deletion; account deletion removes associated records as implemented by backend deletion behavior.

## Security

- Passwords are stored as one-way hashes.
- Auth uses signed access and refresh tokens.
- We apply reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

## User Rights

Users may request to:
- Access their data.
- Correct inaccurate profile data.
- Delete their account and associated data.

## Children

QuestCode JS is not directed to children under 13 without verified parental consent.

## International Transfers

Data may be processed in regions where our infrastructure providers operate, subject to applicable safeguards.

## Contact

For privacy requests and questions, contact: support@questcodejs.com
